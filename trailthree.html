No comic book recommendation list is complete without the granddaddy of all comic books. Watchmen changed the way the world saw superheroes. It remains one of the most influential comic books ever and — despite the so-so movie adaptation and an upcoming HBO series — I’d highly recommend reading the comic. Watchmen is not a ‘cool’ comic book; it’s a visceral upturning of the superhero genre. Between Manhattan and Ozymandias, would you really choose to live in a world with superheroes? Watchmen is what happens when you trust in absolute power, and give your leaders unlimited mandate. Pro-tip: Do not miss the symmetrical issue.
